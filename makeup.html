Simple Makeup Tips For Beginners

Makeup can enhance your natural beauty and boost confidence. Beginners should start with simple and natural makeup routines.

1. Clean Your Skin

Always wash your face before applying makeup to keep skin healthy and fresh.

2. Use Light Foundation

Choose a lightweight foundation for a natural and smooth look.

3. Soft Lip Colors

Pink and nude lipstick shades are perfect for daily makeup.

4. Remove Makeup Properly

Never sleep with makeup on your face. Clean skin prevents acne and dryness.
